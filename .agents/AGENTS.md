# Design Guidelines

- **Color Contrast Rules**: Never use red over pink. Always ensure adequate contrast between backgrounds and interactive elements to prevent eye strain and maintain accessibility.
- **Toggle Components**: All toggles (Tabs, ToggleGroups, custom toggle buttons) must have a transparent/grey background in their inactive state with dark text, and a flush solid blue (`#4b70d8`) background with white text when active. They should not have padding around the active state (flush to the container edges).
